STORY & TEAM

Born in a lab.
Built for the real world.

In 2009, researchers at UC San Diego, working with tuberculosis programs in California and Baja California, Mexico, pioneered the first video directly observed therapy intervention.

Today, SureAdhere is a Dimagi platform in 18 countries, trusted by 350+ programs. The commitment to rigorous evidence and patient-centered care is the same.

Read the full story
2009 – 2015
Research team at UC San Diego pioneers Video Directly Observed Therapy (VDOT) to support TB treatment.
2014 – 2016
SureAdhere commercializes VDOT, bringing the methodology to programs around the world.
2017 – 2018
WHO endorses VDOT, making it a recognized standard of care for TB treatment globally.
2022
SureAdhere joins Dimagi, combining proven digital adherence technology with the world's leading global health platform.
2023
CDC recommends VDOT as an equivalent alternative to in-person DOT for TB treatment.
Managing Director, SureAdhere

Dr. Kelly Collins

As a social entrepreneur and infectious disease epidemiologist, Kelly's career mission has been to support the implementation and scale of evidence-based, high-impact digital health interventions globally. Kelly is the former CEO and Co-Founder of SureAdhere Mobile Technology, which was acquired by Dimagi in February 2022.

Kelly's 12+ years of experience evaluating digital adherence interventions across 5 continents and 25 countries has resulted in 27 publications. She led SureAdhere through two randomized controlled trials evaluating video observed therapy for TB treatment adherence published in the Lancet and JAMA Open: studies which directly informed WHO treatment guidelines in 2017 and 2022.

FAQS

Common questions answered.

Can't find what you need? Visit the full help site or contact our team.

General
What languages does the SureAdhere mobile app support?

SureAdhere supports 16 languages including Amharic, Filipino, Burmese, Portuguese, Chinese, Russian, French, Somali, German, Spanish, Haitian, Tagalog, Kyrgyz, Ukrainian, Marshallese, and Vietnamese. Additional languages can be added on request.

How do I get pricing information?

Pricing is tailored to your program's size and needs. Contact our team for a quote.

Staff Accounts
How do I create a staff account?

Only Client Administrators and Site Staff Administrators can create accounts. Click the green plus icon, select New Staff, fill in the required information, and click Save.

How do I reset my password?

Two options: (1) On the login page, click Forgot your password? and follow the prompts. (2) Within the app, click Reset Password in the upper right corner and provide your username and email for a verification code.

How do I change the Provider App language?

Click the person icon in the top right, select Choose Language, and pick your preference.

Patient Management
How do I create a new patient?

Click the green plus icon, select New Patient, enter the required information, and click Save.

How do I retrieve a patient's PIN?

Two methods: (1) Reset: go to the patient record > Profile tab > scroll down > click Send/Reset PIN. (2) Locate existing: go to the Notifications tab and find the most recent PIN in the list.

How do I deactivate or reactivate a patient?

To deactivate: go to Patients > Profile tab > uncheck Account is active under Account Setup > Save. Reactivation follows the same process in reverse. Note: no data is deleted when deactivating.

Regimens
How do I stop an individual drug in a regimen?

Open the regimen edit tab (click the pencil icon), navigate to the drug schedule, click Stop Drug, select the stop date, and click the main Save button.

Can I edit the days per week a dose is scheduled?

No. You'll need to create a new drug schedule with the updated frequency instead.

What should I do if a patient has a gap in treatment?

It is best to start a new drug schedule to accurately reflect the treatment timeline.

Can I delete a drug or drug schedule?

Deletion is not allowed in order to prevent accidental loss of data. Instead, use the Stop Drug or Stop Drug Schedule button to end it.
