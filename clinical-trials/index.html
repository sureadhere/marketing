// THE CHALLENGE

Bring effective treatments to market faster, for less

Clinical trials for infectious disease treatments often struggle with poor adherence and high dropout rates, hindering treatment outcomes and delaying time-to-market for critical medications. SureAdhere's end-to-end platform tackles these challenges by ensuring adherence to treatment protocols, providing actionable insights, and enabling remote participant engagement, empowering clinical trial sponsors, sites, and CROs to make faster, data-driven decisions about regimen efficacy.

// PLATFORM CAPABILITIES

Everything you need for high-fidelity trials.

Ensure High Adherence to Treatment

Ensure extremely high adherence to treatment regimens with configurable dose reminders and detailed dosing histories, accelerating trials, reducing costs, and delivering top-quality data to confirm regimen efficacy.

  • Configurable dose reminders and complex regimen support
  • Detailed dosing histories and side-effect capture
  • CDC-validated Video Directly Observed Therapy (VDOT)
  • Works offline for participants in low-connectivity settings

Make Confident Decisions

Gain real-time adherence data to support participants, enable timely interventions, and make confident decisions about drug efficacy, all integrated into your trial database.

  • Real-time adherence dashboards for sponsors, CROs, and sites
  • Seamless integration with IRT, EDC, CDR, and MEMs devices
  • Site-level reporting to validate implementation fidelity
  • Audit-ready data logs compliant with 21 CFR Part 11

Engage & Support Participants

Engage and support participants remotely throughout a trial, saving site staff and participant time while reducing dropout. Accessible even in low-literacy or low-connectivity settings.

  • Icon-driven, offline-capable mobile app
  • Secure in-app messaging and live virtual visits
  • Participant-facing treatment calendar and progress tracking
  • Multilingual support for global trial populations

Leading in Privacy & Security

SureAdhere complies with US HIPAA and European GDPR data privacy regulations. The platform is 21 CFR-Part 11 compliant and is regularly audited by trial sponsors and CRO’s. Our global support team provides comprehensive service level agreements based on local time zones.
