WHO WE SERVE

Who SureAdhere Serves.

Providers across the public health, behavioral health, and clinical research spectrum can create a customized treatment experience within SureAdhere and address important nuances in each care delivery approach. SureAdhere is designed to meet the unique demands of clinical practice and research where outpatient support is critical for treatment adherence and patient outcomes.

Public Health

Ensure treatment adherence and streamline support for TB and other infectious disease treatment with CDC-validated video-based Directly Observed Therapy.

MOBILE APP

Easy Video Directly Observed Therapy for Patients

Empower patients to actively participate in their care journey and track progress.

  • Simple & Accessible ExperienceEasy to use for patients of varying technical literacy levels, with offline functionality for uninterrupted support.
  • Personalized Treatment TrackingPatients get visibility into their journey through an intuitive calendar view and dose history.
  • Direct Access to Care TeamsSecure in-app messaging and live video visits keep patients connected with providers.
  • Designed to Support AdherenceReminders, treatment guidance, and engagement tools that help patients stay on track.
WEB PLATFORM

Streamline Complex Treatment Support for Providers

Easily manage patients with complex regimens, reviewing dosing, triaging cases, and engaging for follow-up.

  • Real-Time Adherence AnalyticsMonitor adherence across patient populations with real-time insights that support rapid triage and intervention.
  • Complex Regimen ManagementConfigure dose reminders, track dosing histories, and capture side effects for complex treatment protocols.
  • Virtual Engagement & CommunicationConnect via secure messaging, SMS, and integrated live video visits for timely follow-up.
  • Flexible Integrations & Adherence ToolsSupport video DOT, in-person DOT, MERM devices, and digital pill caps, with EHR and device integrations.
