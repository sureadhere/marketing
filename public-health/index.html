THE CHALLENGE

Patient-Centered Tools for Better Public Health Outcomes

Infectious diseases like tuberculosis, HIV, and hepatitis C demand long, complex treatment regimens, and keeping patients on track is one of the hardest challenges in public health. Missed doses drive drug resistance, treatment failure, and ongoing transmission.

SureAdhere meets patients where they are, replacing in-person clinic visits with flexible, remote adherence support across TB, HIV, ART, and hepatitis C programs, without sacrificing outcomes.

PUBLIC HEALTH AREAS

One platform, many programs.

SureAdhere's proven adherence technology adapts across infectious disease programs, from TB to HIV, PrEP, and hepatitis C.

Tuberculosis (TB)

SureAdhere's CDC-validated Video Directly Observed Therapy (VDOT) delivers equivalent outcomes to in-person DOT, giving patients the flexibility to take their doses from home while providers maintain the oversight needed to ensure treatment completion.

HIV Treatment & Prevention

SureAdhere supports both people living with HIV and those using PrEP to prevent HIV by making treatment and prevention simpler and more accessible. Video directly-observed therapy and real-time data tracking help patients stay consistent while eliminating the need for costly in-person visits.

Hepatitis C

SureAdhere streamlines Hepatitis C treatment by supporting patients in maintaining daily medication routines. With treatment durations now as short as three months and a 95% success rate, SureAdhere ensures patients stay on track while empowering providers to monitor adherence effortlessly.

DEEP-DIVE

Proven Effective for TB Treatment

Tuberculosis (TB) continues to be one of the most challenging infectious diseases affecting over 10 million lives each year globally.

SureAdhere revolutionizes TB care by making the gold standard of treatment – Directly Observed Therapy (DOT) – accessible and affordable for all. Our CDC-validated platform delivers equivalent treatment outcomes to in-person DOT while reducing the burden on patients and providers.

A landmark CDC-led study, published in JAMA Network Open, demonstrated that SureAdhere's video directly observed therapy (VDOT) is as effective as in-person DOT. These findings have shaped global TB treatment guidelines, including recommendations from the WHO and CDC, leading US health departments to adopt VDOT as the standard of care.
